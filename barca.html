Barcelona

The cosmopolitan capital of Spain’s Catalonia region is known for its art, fiestas, architecture and amazing food, seafood and tapas bar

Tapeo del Born

  • Location: El Born
  • Cuisine: Tapas
  • Phone: +34 933 10 16 07
  • Closed: Monday

A young team of highly motivated people, with a huge passion for cooking and for the produce of the land work at Tapeo, a cozy tapas bar, where you'll be able to sample a wide variety of tapas, from the classics to the more elaborate, using age-old recipes. Must try, among all, are veal burguer with foie gras and mayo ceps, “Fideuá” black catalan cuttlefish pasta and Truffled eggs with foie gras and mushrooms

El Rabipelao

  • Location: Gràcia
  • Cuisine: Venezuelan
  • Phone: +34 931 82 50 35
  • Open every day

To say it using their own words "El bar más imitado de Barcelona", the most imitated bar in Barcelona! Retro/Romantic restaurant and bar, serving tasty fresh cocktails and homemade Venuzuelan national dish, Arepas, in the middle of Gràcia, one of my favourite barrio in Barcelona. Arepas are the equivalent of bread in France ro Italy, can be eaten for breakfast, lunch or dinner and be filled with many different types of fillings or toppings.

El Xampanyet

  • Location: El Born
  • Cuisine: Cocktail bars
  • Phone: +34 933 19 70 03
  • Closed: Monday

Located just opposite Tapeo in the central barrio of El Born and just a short walk from Picasso Museum is Barcelona's little gem, El Xampanyet. Old school traditional tapas bar and Xampanyeria (Cava bar - Catalonia's own take on France's Champagne). Great to spend some time with your friend and enjoy some delicious tapas.

La Cova Fumanda

  • Location: Barceloneta
  • Cuisine: Spanish Tapas
  • Phone: +34 932 21 40 61
  • Closed: Sunday

La Coav Fumada (“The Smoked Cave”) is an old school hearty tavern almost unchanged since the 1940s located near by the busy beaches of Barcelonetafamous because is said to be the birthplace of legendary Bombas (Bombs), delicious little spicy potato balls with mince meat. Also a must-try are stewed garbanzos, 'capipota', squid, small octopus and salted cod.
